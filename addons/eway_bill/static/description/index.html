E-way Bill

E-way bill or Electronic-way bill is a document introduced under the GST regime that needs to be generated before transporting or shipping goods within state or inter-state.

Customer Invoice

Create Customer Invoice with all the information.

Outward Picking

Click on Create Picking button to create outward of invoice.

Validate all the quantities of Outward Picking to view Generate Eway Bill button.

Click on Generate Eway Bill button to create Eway Bill with all datas.

Eway Bill generated with all data from Customer Invoice.
